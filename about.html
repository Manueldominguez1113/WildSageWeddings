My name is Bekah Anderson, and I am a certified wedding planner who is excited to help bring your dream a reality!

I decided I do not have an eye for wedding photography, but I do have an eye for details, planning, and helping others create their perfect day!

My favorite part of a wedding is the thoughtful details that represent the couples personality and charm. The details are what set your wedding apart from all the others. I am dedicated and determined to put my all in to each and every wedding that I have the honor of planning or coordinating.
